Key Notes

Just because a houseplant is non-toxic to your pet, it doesn't mean it won't cause digestive upset if eaten. They can still cause stomach cramps, diarrhea and other intestinal problems, so it's always best to try and keep any plant away from your pets. Most smaller plants can be kept in a hanging basket which is a great option.

Remember: Just because the plant is safe for pets, does not mean the pets are "safe" for the plant.
